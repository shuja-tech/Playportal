About

Welcome to our Web-Based Games Website!⭐

This project was born out of a shared passion for gaming and web development, and is presented as part of our university coursework. We aimed to create an engaging and interactive platform where users can enjoy a variety of web-based games.


⭐Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a fun and accessible gaming experience. We believe that games can bring people together and offer a much-needed escape from the daily grind.

Technologies Used To bring this project to life, we utilized several technologies:

    🟠HTML: For structuring the content on our web pages.
    🟠CSS: For styling and making our website visually appealing.
    🟠JavaScript: For adding interactivity and dynamic content to our games.
    🟠Python: For routing and handling backend operations using Flask.
